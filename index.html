Wenhao Li (李文豪)

I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science, Peking University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Liu. Now I am a visiting scholar at the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group, University of Trento, Italy, supervised by Prof. Nicu Sebe.

My current research interest lies in human-centric visual understanding, particularly for 3D human pose and shape estimation in images/videos using deep learning approaches.
