Wenhao Li (李文豪)

I'm currently a final year Ph.D. student (2019.09 - Now) in the School of Computer Science, Peking University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Liu. I was a visiting Ph.D. Student at the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group, University of Trento, Italy, supervised by Prof. Nicu Sebe.

My current research interest focuses on 3D Human Pose Estimation (HPE) and Human Mesh Recovery (HMR) in images or videos using deep learning approaches.
